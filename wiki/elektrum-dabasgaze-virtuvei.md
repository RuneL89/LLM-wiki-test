# Elektrum Dabasgāze Virtuvei

**Summary**: Natural gas tariff plan for kitchen use offered by [[as-latvenergo]], available as an open-ended contract.

**Sources**: Rekins_949553846121.pdf, Rekins_949096401706.pdf, Rekins_949258599751.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-09

---

Elektrum Dabasgāze Virtuvei is a natural gas plan marketed for kitchen use by AS Latvenergo. It is available as an open-ended (beztermiņa) contract.

## Pricing structure

Natural gas bills under this plan include:

1. **Gas energy charge** — per kWh of gas energy
2. **Transmission fee** — for using the transmission grid
3. **Distribution fee** — variable per kWh plus fixed monthly charge
4. **Excise tax** — applied to gas consumption

## Example from bill

From [[rekins-949553846121]] (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf):

| Component | Consumption | Rate | Amount |
|-----------|-------------|------|--------|
| Gas energy | 77.38 kWh | 0.06690 EUR/kWh | 5.18 EUR |
| Transmission | 77.38 kWh | 0.00321 EUR/kWh | 0.25 EUR |
| Distribution (variable) | 77.38 kWh | 0.0091608 EUR/kWh | 0.71 EUR |
| Distribution (fixed) | 1 unit | 6.00 EUR | 6.00 EUR |
| Excise tax | 77.38 kWh | 0.00595 EUR/kWh | 0.46 EUR |

## Unit conversion

The bill shows gas measured in m³ and converted to kWh:
- Volume: 7.00 m³
- Calorific coefficient: 10.838 kWh/m³
- Static pressure coefficient: 1.02
- Energy: 7.00 × 10.838 × 1.02 = 77.38 kWh (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf)

## Tariff changes in 2026

On 1 January 2026, gas distribution and excise tax rates changed as part of a [[gas-distribution-tariff-reform-2026|regulatory reform]]. The example above reflects post-reform rates. Pre-reform distribution rates were approximately 0.02809 EUR/kWh (variable) and 4.62 EUR (fixed) (source: Rekins_949096401706.pdf).
