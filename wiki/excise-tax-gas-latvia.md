# Excise Tax on Natural Gas in Latvia

**Summary**: Excise duty applied to natural gas consumed as fuel in Latvia, calculated per kWh of gas energy.

**Sources**: Rekins_949553846121.pdf, Rekins_949096401706.pdf, Rekins_949258599751.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-09

---

Natural gas used as fuel in Latvia is subject to an excise tax (akcīzes nodoklis) applied to the energy content of the gas consumed.

## Legal basis

The excise tax is applied in accordance with Latvian Cabinet of Ministers Regulations No. 199 of 4 April 2017, "Regulations on the Circulation of Natural Gas and the Procedure for Applying Excise Duty" (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).

## Calculation

The tax is calculated per kWh of gas energy. In [[rekins-949553846121]], the rate was 0.00595 EUR/kWh for 77.38 kWh, resulting in a tax of 0.46 EUR (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).

## Rate change in 2026

The excise tax rate increased effective 1 January 2026:

- **Pre-2026**: 0.00380 EUR/kWh (source: Rekins_949096401706.pdf)
- **Post-2026**: 0.00595 EUR/kWh (source: Rekins_949258599751.pdf)

This change is part of the broader [[gas-distribution-tariff-reform-2026]].
