# VAT on Energy in Latvia

**Summary**: Value-added tax rules for electricity and natural gas services in Latvia, where standard VAT is 21% with certain exemptions.

**Sources**: Rekins_949553846121.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-09

---

In Latvia, energy services are generally subject to standard VAT, but certain related services may be exempt.

## Standard VAT rate

Electricity and natural gas supply, transmission, and distribution services are subject to **21% VAT** (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).

## Exemptions

Insurance services such as [[sos-komplekts]] are classified as non-VAT transactions under Article 52, paragraph 1, clause d.20 of the Latvian Value Added Tax Law (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).

## Example from bill

From [[rekins-949553846121]]:
- VAT-applicable amount: 42.13 EUR
- VAT 21%: 8.85 EUR
- Non-VAT amount: 1.98 EUR (SOS Komplekts)
- Grand total: 52.96 EUR
