# Capacity Fee Reduction 2025

**Summary**: A temporary state-supported reduction applied to the electricity capacity fee for household connections in Latvia during 2025.

**Sources**: Rekins_949096401706.pdf, Rekins_949669121558.pdf, Rekins_949473949511.pdf, Rekins_949149734463_250512_163913.pdf, Rekins_949846089330.pdf, Rekins_949364875616_250808_172750.pdf, Rekins_949234523725_250906_165709.pdf, Rekins_949895009230_251008_102353.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-11

---

During 2025, Latvian electricity bills included a line item *"Maksa par jaudas uzturēšanu (maksas samazinājums 2025. gadā)"* — a capacity fee reduction granted to households.

## Details

The reduction was applied as a negative charge based on the contracted amperage of the connection.

- **Rate**: –0.027999 EUR per ampere (A) per month
- **Example**: For a 20 A connection, the reduction was **–0.56 EUR/month** (source: Rekins_949096401706.pdf)
- **Duration**: Active on bills covering consumption through December 2025

## End of the measure

Bills covering consumption from January 2026 onward no longer include this reduction (source: Rekins_949258599751.pdf). The standard [[capacity-fee]] of 6.80 EUR for a 20 A connection resumed without offset.
