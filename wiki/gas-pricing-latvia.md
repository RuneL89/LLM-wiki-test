# Natural Gas Pricing in Latvia

**Summary**: Structure of household natural gas bills in Latvia, comprising energy, transmission, distribution, and excise tax components.

**Sources**: Rekins_949553846121.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-09

---

Household natural gas bills in Latvia from providers such as [[as-latvenergo]] typically include the following components.

## Components

### 1. Gas energy charge

The cost of the actual gas energy consumed, charged per kWh. The gas volume in m³ is converted to kWh using calorific and pressure coefficients. In the example bill, the rate was 0.06690 EUR/kWh (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).

### 2. Transmission fee

A charge for using the national transmission system. In the example bill, this was 0.00321 EUR/kWh (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).

### 3. Distribution fee

A charge for local distribution, split into:
- **Variable part**: per kWh consumed (0.0091608 EUR/kWh in the example)
- **Fixed part**: monthly flat fee (6.00 EUR in the example)
(source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf)

### 4. Excise tax

An energy tax applied to gas consumption. In the example bill, this was 0.00595 EUR/kWh, totaling 0.46 EUR (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf). See [[excise-tax-gas-latvia]] for details.

## Unit conversion

Gas meters typically record volume in cubic metres (m³). Bills convert this to energy (kWh) using:
- **Calorific coefficient**: e.g., 10.838 kWh/m³
- **Static pressure coefficient**: e.g., 1.02

## VAT

Gas energy and distribution services are subject to [[vat-energy-latvia|21% VAT]].
