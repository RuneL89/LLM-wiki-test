# SOS Komplekts

**Summary**: Optional insurance and service package offered by [[as-latvenergo]] as an add-on to energy contracts.

**Sources**: Rekins_949553846121.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-09

---

SOS Komplekts is an optional insurance and service package that AS Latvenergo customers can add to their electricity or gas contract.

## Pricing

In [[rekins-949553846121]], the monthly fee was 1.98 EUR (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).

## Tax treatment

The SOS Komplekts fee is not subject to VAT. According to the bill, this is because insurance is a non-VAT transaction under Article 52, paragraph 1, clause d.20 of the Latvian Value Added Tax Law (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).
