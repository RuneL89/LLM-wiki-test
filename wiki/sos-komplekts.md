# SOS Komplekts

**Summary**: Optional insurance and service package offered by [[as-latvenergo]] as an add-on to energy contracts.

**Sources**: Rekins_949553846121.pdf, Rekins_949149734463_250512_163913.pdf, Rekins_949846089330.pdf, Rekins_949744845142.pdf, Rekins_949364875616_250808_172750.pdf, Rekins_949234523725_250906_165709.pdf, Rekins_949895009230_251008_102353.pdf, Rekins_949096401706.pdf, Rekins_949669121558.pdf, Rekins_949473949511.pdf, Rekins_949258599751.pdf, Rekins_949329365476.pdf, Rekins_949206612654.pdf

SOS Komplekts is an optional insurance and service package that AS Latvenergo customers can add to their electricity or gas contract.

## Pricing

In [[rekins-949553846121]], the monthly fee was 1.98 EUR (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf). The same fee appears consistently across all observed bills from April 2025 through April 2026.

## Tax treatment

The SOS Komplekts fee is not subject to VAT. According to the bill, this is because insurance is a non-VAT transaction under Article 52, paragraph 1, clause d.20 of the Latvian Value Added Tax Law (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf).
