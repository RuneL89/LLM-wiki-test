# Elektrum Ekonomiskais

**Summary**: Single-zone electricity tariff plan offered by [[as-latvenergo]] for household customers.

**Sources**: Rekins_949553846121.pdf, Rekins_949096401706.pdf, Rekins_949669121558.pdf, Rekins_949473949511.pdf, Rekins_949258599751.pdf, Rekins_949329365476.pdf, Rekins_949206612654.pdf

**Last updated**: 2026-05-09

---

Elektrum Ekonomiskais is a single-zone ([[single-zone-tariff]]) electricity plan offered by AS Latvenergo. It is available as an open-ended (beztermiņa) contract.

## Pricing structure

The bill separates electricity costs into three components:

1. **Energy charge** — per kWh consumed
2. **Delivery fee** — per kWh for grid usage
3. **Capacity fee** — based on contracted amperage

## Example from bill

From [[rekins-949553846121]] (source: Rekins_949553846121.pdf):

| Component | Consumption | Rate | Amount |
|-----------|-------------|------|--------|
| Energy (1 laika zonā) | 144.23 kWh | 0.11800 EUR/kWh | 17.02 EUR |
| Delivery fee | 144.23 kWh | 0.03962 EUR/kWh | 5.71 EUR |
| Capacity fee | 20 A | 0.34 EUR/A | 6.80 EUR |
